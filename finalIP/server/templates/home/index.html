About

StartupIdea is a free service that allows users to submit ideas about "Start-up" companies. A start-up idea includes information about the title of the idea, a short description (a couple of paragraphs), the industry or market it belongs to from a list of at least 5 pre-defined choices (health, technology, education, finance, travel). Registered user has the privilege of navigating through startup ideas submitted by other users and provide their opinions with like/dislike.

Whether you're a student looking to start your own business or have a brilliant idea that you would want other users to know and collaborate with or a professional looking to attract clients, StartupIdea is the perfect starting point!
